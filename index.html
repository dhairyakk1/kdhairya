Who I Am

I'm Dhairya Kant Kislay, a B.Tech student in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi. I have a passion for turning complex problems into elegant, purposeful software.

My work spans Python-based tooling, climate data visualization, AI chatbots, and data deduplication pipelines. I've competed in national-level hackathons like Smart India Hackathon and Technex '26 (IIT-BHU), shipping production-grade projects under tight deadlines.

Beyond projects, I'm deeply interested in competitive programming and quantitative finance — drawn to the algorithmic rigour of the former and the mathematical depth of the latter. I code primarily in C++ and Python, and care about building from first principles — not copy-pasted from templates.
